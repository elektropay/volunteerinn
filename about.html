Who are we?

The Institute of Modern Spanish was founded in 1995. We provide top quality educational programs – specializing in Spanish language and Mexican culture instruction and immersion. As part of our dedication to learning through serving, we have offered volunteer opportunities to students since 2005. Through our dedicated staff, we provide quality support to volunteers in a variety of programs. A decade of feedback attests to our students’ top-notch experiences in volunteering and cultural immersion. Based on this – now through Volunteer Inn Mexico – we have opened our doors to volunteers from all walks of life, to people who want to learn and serve while living in Mexico.

Why volunteer with Volunteer Inn?

Volunteers tell us Volunteer Inn Mexico creates an unforgettable experience for them in Mexico. Volunteer Inn Mexico builds on strong and trustworthy relationships with the many needs-based organizations that seek us out for help from teachers (especially in English), childcare services, healthcare support, and in environmental and ecological projects, as well as animal and vet services, and other organizations worthy of our assistance. For volunteers who come to us, Volunteer Inn Mexico provides 24/7 support, including housing, and meals.

While paying a service fee to do volunteer work may sound counterintuitive, with Volunteer Inn, clients who come to us are assured of well organized programs in centers of bona-fide community need and high interest to participants. Client safety and health are priorities for us. Volunteers live and eat with like-minded volunteers from all over the world. Our in-country, dual-language support team guide clients throughout a volunteer’s stay in Mexico.
